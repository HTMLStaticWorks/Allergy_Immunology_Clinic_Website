Frequently Asked Questions

Does allergy testing hurt?

Skin prick testing generally feels like a small scratch on the surface of the skin. It is very tolerable, even for young children. There are no needles involved in standard skin testing.

How long do I need to be off antihistamines?

You must stop all oral antihistamines (like Zyrtec, Claritin, Allegra, Benadryl) for 5 to 7 days prior to standard allergy skin testing to avoid false negative results.

Do you treat pediatric patients?

Absolutely. We treat patients of all ages, adapting our testing and treatment approaches to be child-friendly and stress-free.

Our Facility

Our Philosophy of Care

Our approach is built on a foundation of core principles that guide every patient interaction and treatment decision.

Evidence-Based Medicine

We adhere strictly to treatments and diagnostic methods that are backed by rigorous scientific research and proven outcomes.

Patient Partnership

You are a key member of your care team. We believe in shared decision-making and empowering you with knowledge.

Continuous Learning

Our providers are committed to lifelong learning, constantly integrating the latest advancements in immunology into our practice.

Compassionate Care

We understand the physical and emotional toll of allergic diseases and provide care with empathy and respect.

The AllergyCare Way

Our process is fine-tuned to ensure a seamless, supportive, and effective patient experience from start to finish.

1. Detailed Intake

We start with a comprehensive review of your history and symptoms before you even see the doctor.

2. Collaborative Diagnosis

Our providers meet to discuss complex cases, ensuring you benefit from our collective expertise.

3. Actionable Plan

We provide you with a clear, written action plan that outlines your treatment, goals, and expectations.

4. Proactive Follow-up

Our team proactively checks in to monitor your progress and ensure your treatment is on track.

Our History & Community Involvement

Growing with our community and supporting local asthma education programs in schools.

2006

Clinic Founded

Dr. Jenkins opened the first single-room clinic focused primarily on pediatric food allergies.

2012

Facility Expansion

Moved to our current state-of-the-art facility, adding specialized PFT testing rooms and the immunotherapy compounding lab.

2018

School Outreach Program

Launched our community initiative teaching local school nurses how to manage Anaphylaxis Action Plans and EpiPen administration.

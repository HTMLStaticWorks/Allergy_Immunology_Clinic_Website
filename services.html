Clinical Offerings

Advanced diagnostics paired with modern therapies to treat the root cause of your symptoms.

Diagnostic Testing

Precise identification of your triggers.

Skin Prick Testing

The gold standard for identifying environmental and food allergies. Results are readable in 15-20 minutes during your visit.

Patch Testing

Identifies contact dermatitis triggers (metals, chemicals, cosmetics). Patches are worn for 48 hours to check for delayed reactions.

PFTs (Spirometry)

Pulmonary Function Tests to measure lung capacity and identify asthma. Done quickly and painlessly in-office.

Treatments & Therapies

Customized plans for long-term health.

Immunotherapy

Slowly building your immune tolerance to specific allergens to permanently reduce symptoms.

  • SCIT: Traditional allergy shots
  • SLIT: Under-the-tongue drops (Sublingual)

Biologic Therapies

Advanced injectable medications that target specific immune system pathways.

  • For Severe Asthma (Xolair, Fasenra)
  • For Chronic Hives & Eczema (Dupixent)

Specialized care for children, including oral food challenges and comprehensive documentation for school forms and action plans.

The Healing Steps

Our structured approach is designed to guide you from uncertainty and discomfort to clarity and lasting wellness.

1. Initial Evaluation

A comprehensive discussion about your symptoms, medical history, and treatment goals.

2. Diagnostic Phase

State-of-the-art testing to uncover the precise cause of your symptoms with maximum accuracy.

3. Strategic Plan

Development of a personalized, evidence-based treatment strategy tailored to your diagnosis.

4. Active Management

Continuous care and partnership to manage your condition effectively for the long term.
